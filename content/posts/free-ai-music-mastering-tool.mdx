## Why AI Music Needs Mastering

Suno and Udio export tracks at -10 to -12 LUFS — significantly louder than what streaming platforms expect. Spotify targets -14 LUFS. Apple Music targets -16 LUFS.

When you upload an unmastered AI track, the platform turns it down automatically. But turning down a clipped track doesn't fix clipping. The distortion stays — just at a lower volume.

Mastering fixes this before upload.

## Three Modes — Pick What Fits

**Quick** — one click, done in seconds. Applies a high-pass filter, true peak limiter, and normalizes to your platform target. Best for most AI tracks.

**Auto-Master** — analyzes your track automatically. Detects genre, selects the right processing chain, applies corrections where needed. Choose your platform and processing strength.

**Manual** — full control. 8-band parametric EQ, parallel compressor, tape saturation, de-esser, stereo widening, reverb. For when you want to go deeper.

1. Open [wavedisco.com/studio](https://wavedisco.com/studio)
